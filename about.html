Building digital confidence — one student at a time.

Since 2020, Smart Computer Training Center has been the trusted computer learning hub of Kahla, Bihar.

A small-town institute with big ambitions.

We started with one goal — to bring the same quality of computer education available in cities to students in our village. Today, we are proud to have trained 500+ students from across Saran district, many of whom now work in banking, government departments, retail billing, and freelance design.

Our trainers focus on practical learning. Every student gets dedicated computer time, real-life assignments, and one-on-one doubt clearing — because that's how skills are built.
